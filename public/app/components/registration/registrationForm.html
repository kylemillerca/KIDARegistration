By checking the box below, I agree to adhere to the previous policies in order to compete during the 2015 competitive season. I understand that violation of any of these policies and procedures may result in disqualification from any or all KIDA events and/or financial penalty to my ensemble.
Members may make revisions to their show registration without penalty between December 1 and January 1. Any member unit canceling after January 1 may lose their bond to the show host running the show. Any member unit canceling 2 to 4 weeks before a show date may lose their bond plus be subject to an additional $50.00 fine to the show host running the show. Any member unit canceling 1 week or less before a show date may lose their bond and be subject to an additional $100 fine to the show host running the show and will go on first in Championships within their given class. The show fee will be forfeited for any cancelation after January 1. The cancellation policy will be enforced at the discretion of the Contest Coordinator and President when no legitimate reason is given for the cancellation.
